Mac Studios and Mac Minis are powerful machines — but they have no built-in microphone. You can't dictate in Notes, Slack, or any app without plugging in a headset or walking across the room.

"I have a three-computer setup. My Mac Studio runs everything, but every time I needed to type something, I had to stop what I was doing. I just wanted to speak and have the text show up."

ReMyk solves this by borrowing the mic from a nearby laptop. Speak into your laptop's browser, and the text auto-pastes into the active app on your Mac Studio. Over WiFi. In about 2 seconds.

Features

Built for the Three-Computer Workflow

Laptop #1 provides the mic. Mac Studio runs the server. Laptop #2 stays free for research.

🔒

Zero Cloud

Everything runs on your hardware. Whisper AI on the Mac Studio, mic capture in the browser. No data ever leaves your LAN.

⚡

~2 Second Round Trip

Browser-native audio decode + CPU Whisper + optimized AppleScript. 12× faster than the legacy ffmpeg path.

🌐

Browser-Based Client

No app install on the client. Open any browser on the LAN, tap the mic button, and speak. Works on any OS.

🔄

Auto-Start via launchd

Server starts on login automatically. Never think about it again — just open the URL and dictate.

🔊

Raw PCM Transport

Audio is decoded and resampled in the browser via Web Audio API, then sent as raw Int16 PCM. No temp files, no ffmpeg, no subprocesses.

💰

Free & Open Source

MIT licensed. Self-hosted. No subscriptions, no usage limits, no hidden costs. Just Whisper on your own hardware.

Getting Started

Up and Running in 2 Minutes

One command on your Mac Studio. One URL in your browser.

Install Dependencies

On your Mac Studio: pip install -r requirements.txt

Start the Server

Run python server/listen.py — it pre-loads Whisper and starts on port 8765.

Open the URL

From any laptop: https://<mac-studio-ip>:8765

Speak

Tap the mic, speak, tap again. Text auto-pastes into the active app on the Mac.
